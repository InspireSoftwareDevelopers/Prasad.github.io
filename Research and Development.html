As a SPECIAL OFFICER(EVALUATION)

Calendar of events

Draw up a Calendar of events in consultation with Directors of all Schools for various examinations and notify in the beginning of each Academic year.

Board of Examiners (BOEs)

Prepare list of examiners submitted by respective Board of Studies and place the same before the BOAE to constitute the Board of Examiners (BOEs) and panel of examiners as per the provisions under REVA University Statutes relating to formation of the Board of Examiners and their function.

Guidelines

Ensure that the question papers are prepared as per REVA University guidelines already in place.

University Regulation

Arrange for setting question papers and conduct of the meetings of various Boards of Examiners (BOEs) for scrutinizing the question papers as per the provisions under the University Regulations, through the Chairpersons of respective Boards of Examiners.

Integrity and Confidentiality

Take necessary measures to facilitate the needs of the physically challenged and Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) students without affecting the integrity and confidentiality of the examination system.

Fair valuation

Be responsible for arranging fair valuation of the answer scripts of examinations held in all subjects in cooperation with internal and external examiners.

Identification and Appointing

Handling Ph.D related matters,identification and appointing examiners,conduction of viva voce,etc.

Publish Results

Finalize and publish results of students of all programs within the stipulated period after obtaining approval from the Registrar-Evaluation/Vice-Chancellor.

Analysis

Review the results and prepare analysis of all programs and present the same before the Authorities of the Universities after obtaining approval from the Registrar-Evaluation/ViceChancellor.

Duties and Responsibilities

Prepare periodically the list of those paper setters, examiners, evaluators, moderators, invigilators, and other persons who have committed lapses in discharging the examination related duties and responsibilities entrusted to them and refer them to the enquiry committee of the University constituted for the purpose, after obtaining approval of the RegistrarEvaluation/Vice-Chancellor; and thereafter place the recommendations of the Committee before the Board of Governors for taking appropriate action against them as per the provisions under the Regulations.

MPLEC

Prepare periodically the list of those students who have involved in malpractices and unethical practices in the examinations; Refer this list of students to the Malpractices Lapses Enquiry Committee (MPLEC) of the University after obtaining the approval of the Registrar-EvaluationVice-Chancellor..

Reforms

Initiate continuous reforms in examination related activities and implement them in true spirit.

Confidentiality

Be responsible for maintenance of confidentiality in examination related works.
